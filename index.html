Popular Places

Maldives
Pad barefoot along dazzling beaches under leaning palms. Enjoy water so clear that boats look as though they are levitating, and sand so white and fine that it pours through your hands like milk. Every island is wreathed with reefs; every day is close to perfect – and all you’ll need to remember is where you last left your shoes. Welcome to the Maldives.
Thailand
With 16 million foreigners flying into the country each year, Thailand is Asia’s primary travel destination and offers a host of places to visit. Travel to Thailand and you’ll find that despite this vast influx of visitors, it’s cultural integrity remains largely undamaged. Thailand is a country that cleverly avoided colonisation has been able to absorb Western influences while maintaining its own rich heritage.
Indonesia
With over 17,000 islands spanning from South East Asia across to Oceania, you are spoilt for choice. The ever popular Bali became a second home for me and I discovered there is a good and the bad side to it. You can find remote paradise in Raja Ampat, explore some of the world’s most famous temples in Yogyakarta and be amazed at a million other things.
